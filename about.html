About This Website

Welcome to

Fake News vs Real News! This website was developed to help people identify fake news, understand the characteristics of real news, and improve media literacy.

Our goal is to provide easy-to-understand guidance, tips, and examples that empower users to spot misinformation online. Whether you are a student, teacher, or just curious about the news, this platform is here to educate and inform.

Developer Information

This website was created by Zedrix T. and Josh G. from Asian Institute of Computer Studies. You can reach out via email at jjemonmark@gmail.com .

Why We Share This Information

The internet is full of information, but not all of it is accurate. By sharing tools, examples, and tips on spotting fake news, we aim to promote critical thinking and responsible consumption of media.

We believe that an informed society is a stronger society. This platform is free to use and share with anyone who wants to learn more about spotting misinformation.
